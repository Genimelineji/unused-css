How it Works.

Beatrio.com is a product of EmployeeStack Inc. a 501(3)c registered in Kentucky whose mission is to help advance the careers of U.S. Military Veterans, Troops, Prior Service Members & Military Souses. EmployeeStack Inc. donates all fund earned through Beatrio.com and distributes them as grants through VetStack.org.

Signing Up is Free. Only Registered Users can make purchases, sell services, host events, post jobs and post resumes on Beatrio.com. Anyone can use the Beatrio Professional Network, Hiring Marketplace & Events Platform , however U.S. Military Veterans, Troops, Prior Service and Military Spouses receive a significant discount.
