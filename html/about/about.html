About.

Beatrio.com is a product of EmployeeStack Inc. a 501(3)c registered in Kentucky whose mission is to help advance the careers of U.S. Military Veterans, Troops, Prior Service Members & Military Souses. EmployeeStack Inc. donates all fund earned through Beatrio.com and distributes them as grants through VetStack.org.

Beatrio.com is a Professional Network, hiring Marketplace and Events Center for Musicians.

Beatrio.com is a Professional Network for Musicians in which everyone is welcome and encouraged to join.

The website Beatrio.com is a product of EmployeeStack Inc., a 501(3)c whose mission is to help U.S. Military Veterans, Troops, Prior Service Members (with honorable discharge) and Military Spouses advance their careers in the music industry.

The net revenue from Beatrio will be donated to VetStack.org, our sister site. Where U.S. Military Veterans, Troops, Prior Service Members (with honorable discharge) and Military Spouses can:

--apply for various trainings
--request software
--apply for conferences
--apply for internships with music labels and bands
--work alongside well known artists
--and more...

As mentioned, anyone can use the Beatrio.com Professional Networking Platform for those in the Music Industry. We charge a small fee much like many other "unspecialized general freelancing platforms". Our fees are distributed as grants to helping U.S. Military Veterans, Troops, Prior Service Members (with honorable discharge) and Military Spouses advance their careers in the music industry.

Beatrio offer several products for Musicians to sell to their fan bases:

--Hire an Artist, Band, DJ or Musician
--Hire an Event Location, Event Host or Runner
--Host an Event, Host a Concert
--Host a Professional Network, Join a Fan Group, Interact with other artists
--Post your Music Resume, Hire an artists Browse Music Resumes
--and more...

We also offer significant U.S. Military Veteran, Troop, Prior Service Member and Military Spouse discounts.

For more information see: Payments & Fees.

Our Mission

Supplying Veterans with Grants to achieve career advancement.

EmployeeStack uses the profits generated from the Beatrio.com, to distribute to U.S. Military Veterans, Troops, Prior Service Members (with honorable discharge) and Military Spouses at VetStack.org. This money will be given in the form of grants with the goal of increasing career advancement and employment opportunities.
