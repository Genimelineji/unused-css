What We Do.

Beatrio.com is a product of EmployeeStack Inc. a 501(3)c registered in Kentucky whose mission is to help advance the careers of U.S. Military Veterans, Troops, Prior Service Members & Military Souses. EmployeeStack Inc. donates all fund earned through Beatrio.com and distributes them as grants through VetStack.org.

What does Beatrio.com do? And Why is it a 501(3)c NonProfit.

Beatrio.com is a product of EmployeeStack Inc. and a 501(3)c Non profit registered In Kentucky. Our mission is to advance the skills of U.S. Military Veterans, Troops, Prior Service Members & Military Spouses within the music industry.

Generating profits from music and using it to advance a community

Our goal is to offer a top notch Marketplace, Professional Network, Events Network, Concert & Experience Center and Community for Musicians and fans of Music the funds we raise will go to VetStack.org.

Beatrio.com Musician Portfolio and Resume.

The Beatrio.com Platform for Professional Musicians allows Artists, Bands, DJs, Event Hosts, Event Locations, Mixers, Musicians, Music Layer, Producers, Runners and more... to create an online Resume and portfolio complete with Bio, Photo, videos, reviews, skills, education and more.

Beatrio.com is a platform that allows the user to choose musical service that is right for them.

Beatrio.com is a ran as a service / platform - we do not sell services, nor do we compete with the artists on our platform. The best way to be sucessful on Beatrio.com is to create a nice resume, offer a great service, be good to your customers and thus earn great reviews.

Beatrio.com Events, Parties, Meet-Ups & Concerts.

Beatrio.com respects all users and members of our community. Therefore we created an Events feature that allows Artists & Bands to have Launch Parties. It allows Fans to have Fan Group Meet-Ups, It allows Bars to have "specials nights," -- It allows family & friends to virtually RSVP to a wedding and it allows Musicians to directly sell concerts tickets to fans. This allows Artists to ensure "true fans" are the ones who are getting the opportunity to purchase their tickets.

Buy Concert Tickets directly from the artist! Through Beatrio.com.

As mentioned, EmployeeStack Inc. is a Nonprofit organization, we are not a for profit company and we certainly are not publicly traded like others in this space who charge larger fees due to their fiduciary duty to earn max value for their shareholders. It is our focus as a business to provide users with a platform that maximizes value for musicians, and donate all net revenue to advancing the careers of U.S Military Veterans, Troops, Prior Service Members and Military Spouses. Beatrio.com Fan Groups, Professional Networks & Private Groups.

As a Musician it is important to always be connecting, building networks, meeting other musicians and to promoting your services.

Build your Professional Network through Beatrio.com Networks & Groups.

Beatrio.com Networks is a Professional Networking tool that allows users to communicate online with others through the use of User Groups. You can create a Public Group, which allows you to communicate and network with other artists or fans of the same artist. However you can also enter a Private group which allows you to collaborate privately with other artists, band members, DJ's, Mixers, Producers, Music Lawyers and more to create and generate ideas.
